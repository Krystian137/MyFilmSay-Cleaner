Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Register" button in the navigation menu and fill out the registration form with your name, email, and password. Once registered, you'll be able to start reviewing movies immediately!

How do I find a specific movie?

Use the search bar in the navigation menu to search for movies by title, director, or genre. You can also browse all movies on the homepage and sort them by title, rating, or release date.

How does the rating system work?

Movies display an average rating from all user reviews. When you write a review, you can rate the movie on a scale of 0-10.
